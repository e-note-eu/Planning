# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

**Last Updated:** May 24, 2024

## 1. Privacy Policy

### 1.1 Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how the **Task Planner** application (the "App") handles your information. As a developer, I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a secure and transparent manner.

### 1.2 Data Collection and Use
**The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
- No user accounts are required to use the App.
- No personal identifiers (such as name, email, or phone number) are collected.
- No usage analytics or tracking data (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) are integrated into the App.

### 1.3 Data Storage
All data created within the App (tasks, descriptions, and completion timestamps) is stored **locally on your device** using an encrypted Room/SQLite database. 
- This data never leaves your device.
- I do not have access to your tasks or any other content you create in the App.
- Deleting the App will result in the permanent deletion of all locally stored task data.

### 1.4 Third-Party Services
The App does not share any data with third-party services. There are no advertisements or external SDKs that track user behavior.

### 1.5 EU GDPR Compliance
Since all data remains on your physical device, you have full control over your data as per the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You can access, modify, or delete your data at any time directly through the App interface.
